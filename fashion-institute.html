Lois Apparel Fashion Institute

Training the next generation of fashion creatives with practical, hands-on learning.

What students learn

• Basic and advanced sewing
• Pattern drafting and cutting
• Finishing and neatness
• Simple business mindset for fashion

Classes are structured, practical and supervised so that students complete real garments and build confidence with the machine.

Who can apply

Teenagers, youths and adults who have a genuine interest in fashion – from complete beginners to those who want to sharpen their skills.
