Lois Apparel Fashion Institute

Training the next generation of fashion creatives.

Beyond sewing for clients, Lois Apparel invests in young people – teaching them how to design, cut and finish garments with excellence.

The Lois Apparel Fashion Institute is a structured training space where students learn everything from basic stitches to full garment construction, pattern drafting, fabric selection and finishing.

Students practise on real outfits, develop confidence and graduate with practical skills they can use to build their own fashion businesses or support existing brands.
