About Lois Apparel

A fashion house built on quiet confidence.

Lois Apparel is a Lagos-based fashion brand founded by Bukola Olajubu. Our focus is simple: create pieces that make women feel at ease and elegant – without unnecessary drama.

Every Lois piece begins with a conversation. We listen to how you like your clothes to sit on your body, the events you attend, what you’re comfortable showing and what you’d rather keep soft and subtle.

From that conversation, we sketch, take measurements and source fabrics that match your personality – from structured corporate looks to soft, flowing occasion wear. The result is clothing that feels like you, only more polished.

About Bukola Olajubu

Bukola is a designer and pattern cutter with a deep love for modest, structured silhouettes. Over the years, she has dressed brides, professionals and everyday women who value neat work and reliability.

Her approach is calm and intentional – no shouting, no pressure. Just excellence, delivered with grace.
