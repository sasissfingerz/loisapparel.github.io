About Lois Apparel

A Lagos-based fashion brand led by designer Bukola Olajubu, focused on quiet, intentional elegance.

Our philosophy

Lois Apparel believes clothes should help you feel calm, confident and fully yourself. We favour clean lines, good structure and silhouettes that honour the body.

Each piece is thoughtfully cut, fitted and finished in a way that respects both your time and your comfort. No last-minute panic sewing, no unnecessary drama – just steady excellence.

About the designer

Bukola Olajubu is a fashion designer and pattern cutter with a passion for modest, sharp silhouettes. Over the years she has dressed brides, professionals and everyday women who value neat work and reliability.

What clients love

• Honest guidance on what suits their frame.
• Clear timelines for fittings and collection.
• Clean finishing, inside and out.
• A peaceful, respectful approach to every project.
